"Can Machines Think?"
------------------------------
Substrate Neutral
Physical Symbol System Hypothesis (newell + simon): any system capable of manipulating symbolic data - adding numbers, rearranging lists of symbols, replacing symbols, etc is capable of "intelligence" (top down)

Substrate Dependent
(John Searle) - thinking only occurs in protein based "machine" i.e. biochemical

Subsymbolic Processing - processing of signals (neural networks, bottom up)

parallel vs serial processing
binary vs fuzzy logic
neuron > switch

empiricism - all knowledge is rooted in sensory perception
(linking symbolic processes to sensors)
