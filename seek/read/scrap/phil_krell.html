• The Desert
escaping the cave

so used to the light
being delivered from but one source
a tiny portal
at the end of a long path
that brings back
the troubling idea of movement
space
the brazzen confidence
required to walk
not just through a pathway
but into the sun
the drippings of waxen wings
dripping, searing
but egging on
the walk is not singular
being engulfed in light
but one must compose
keep himself together
to return, expose, to teach
to do what
is right

-phil krell (3/12/16)
