• The Desert
we travellers of the metaverse
do declare that no crevice shall remain
untouched
unexplored
unmapped
unnamed

to roam reality in its endless shades
and rest in realms with a thousand suns
in the shade
of trees unknown
is the only calling
the only vocation that will
cool the flame
within the breast
that seeks to know
and feeds on forms

we dwellars of the cave
